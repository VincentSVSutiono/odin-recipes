Beans and Toast

Description

Beans and toast are a classic British comfort food that has never really taken hold here in the states. Traditionally, canned baked beans served over toasted bread, it is a hearty lunch or part of breakfast, or even dinner, often served with thick "chips." And if you didn't grow up with beans and toast, there is likely nothing about that description that is making you jump up to go grab a can of Bush's.Beans and toast are a classic British comfort food that has never really taken hold here in the states.
